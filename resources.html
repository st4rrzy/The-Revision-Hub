Resources and Tip

Explore the tips and resources we have complied in the site!

What is Exam Stress?

Everyone experience some type of exam stress or exam anxiety. In this page it will explain what is exam stress, what cause it, why people have it and how to overcome it

How to prepare well?

Preparing for an exam is a crucial step into excelling in an exam. It is very important to prepare the right way that suite your capabilities and personal learning style. This page will explore various methods you can revise and how to optimise the time you have.

How to not choke?

Having extreme anxiety may lead to having a blackout (or some may call "choke") in an exam. This can affect individual drastically as it wont show their full potential. This page will discuss what may lead up to a blackout to exam, how to prevent it and how to overcome it if you happen to get a blackout.

Exam Results

When results comes out, it may be very stressful and overwhelming to students. You may know what measures to take after receiving or you may not know what to do at all. It is also important to reminds students that results does not always define who they are as a person. This page will explore how to overcome anxiety leading up to exam results, and what measures you can take.

How the school can help!

This page will discuss ways or measures the school can do to help you during exam season. It is equally important to share with the school if you're experiencing exam stress as they will be there to support you.
