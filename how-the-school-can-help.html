How the school can help!

Going through exam stress alone can be very challenging. It is important to keep in mind that staffs at school can help! Whether it is your teachers or pastoral/wellbeing team, they can help you. Talking to someone about your worries can do wonders!

"...Whenever there is summatives or formatives, I get so stressed that I score grades lower than my expected level. It's really frustrating..."

-Anonymous Student

Steps you can take!

Here are actions you can do to reach out for help.

Ask for your subject teacher assistance

Most worries comes from not being confident of materials/skills needed for an exam. You can go to your subject teacher to solidify your skill.

Reach out to Pastoral/Wellbeing team

You reach you to the school pastoral or wellbeing team if you're experiencing lots of anxiety and stress. They are trained to help you!

Talk to your coordinator

If you feel the stress or anxiety will majorly impact the exams you will take, you can talk to your coordinator to see if you can get arrangement to help!
